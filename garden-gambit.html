Garden Gambit

Team size: 13

Duration: 6 weeks

Softwares: Unity & C#

Assignment: Create a collectible card mobile game with gacha, fusion and upgrading mechanics. Implement rewarded ads and in-app purchases.

My contributions
● Created a flexible and generic inventory system, implemented with inheritance and interfaces, that was used in several parts of the game. Responsible for the full functionality of the players two inventories; the fighter card inventory and the seeds and planted seeds inventory. The generic inventory base was also used by another team member for the shops inventory.

● Created fighter cards using MVC architecture.

● Responsible for the chain of planting seeds, letting them grow and then spawning fighters from them. This includes serialization of data, implementing spawning logic, UI updates and communications across systems.

● Used the Broker Pattern throughout and implemented messages.
